COVID-19

The Gene Ontology Consortium is gathering annotations and models about the Coronavirus COVID-19 to help understand its mechanisms of infection.

Ongoing work of the GO Consortium

  • Integrating the COVID-19 genes to allow for the curation and reuse of recent articles on COVID-19.
  • Producing GO-CAMs to further detail the infectious mechanisms of COVID-19.
  • Providing interactive jupyter notebooks to learn how to rapidly use and integrate GO data in larger analysis.
