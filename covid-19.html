SARS-CoV-2 - Coronavirus

This is a work in progress and users should expect the data provided here to evolve soon.
Last Update: March 30, 2020

The Gene Ontology Consortium is gathering annotations and models about the SARS-CoV-2 virus to help understand and share on the mechanisms of infection of the COVID-19 disease. This page summarizes and give quick access to some of the information we gathered.
See why differentiate SARS-CoV-2 and COVID-19 ?

SARS-CoV-2 cell entry

The virus has been shown to require two human proteins (ACE2, TMPRSS2) for cell entry1,2.

    Ongoing work of the GO Consortium

    • Integrating the SARS-CoV-2 genes to allow for the curation and reuse of recent articles on SARS-CoV-2.
    • Producing GO-CAMs to further detail the infectious mechanisms of SARS-CoV-2.
    • Providing interactive jupyter notebooks to learn how to rapidly use and integrate GO data in larger analysis.
